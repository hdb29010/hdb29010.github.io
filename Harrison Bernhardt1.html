About Me

Hello, I am a student at the University of Georgia and I am majoring in Real Estate. I am from Atlanta, Georgia and I intend to return there for work after college. I'm looking for a job in commercial real estate, hopefully in a brokering firm where i can put together deals for major companies by finding them office spaces all over Metro Atlanta, Buckhead, and most of The greater Atlanta Regions.

My Family

I am a big family man, I love my family. I have my two parents Jim and Jill Bernhardt who also had my brother 3 years before they had me. My Brothers name is Garrett Bernhardt and he now works for the PGA in West Palm Beach. I grew up very close to him and i still am pretty close with him. He travels all over the country and always brings me something back from the cities he visits. My mother works for a radio station and manages advertisement while my dad works for an IT company and works with sales to get the software out to its customers. I don't have a very large family but I wouldn't trade them for the world.

My High School

I went to High school at St. Pius X Catholic High school, It is a catholic school in Atlanta Georgia. I played two sports through out my 4 years including Football and Golf. I played offense and defense in football and enjoyed my time there because it taught me discipline and showed me what hard work does. I went to Georgia dome twice which was an unbelievable experience to play wheres the pros play. Unfortunately, we lost both times we went to state but it was still a once and a life time experience.
